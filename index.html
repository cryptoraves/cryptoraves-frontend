The Potential of a Tokenized Economy

Many blockchain projects have been launched to tokenize real world assets, such as real estate, fine art, music, and so on. Want a personal stake in the new high rise condo complex down the street?  Or have fractional ownership in a famous Picasso painting?  That is literally happening today, with tokens that have already been launched on Ethereum.  New assets, or those previously available only to the wealthy elite, will soon be a part of anyones portfolio.  And these enhanced portfolios will be more diverse than ever before.

Cryptoraves Brings Blockchain to the Masses

Cryptoraves aims to capture new value by tokenizing social credibility, while removing technical and psychological barriers  to blockchain adoption by the general public. The idea:  Launch your own crypto and spread it through social media.  Were targeting Twitter, and are focused on giving any Twitter user this ability without the burden of wallet and key management.  


Example: Twitter user @Bob can use our Dapp to launch himself 1 billion @Bob tokens. He can then Tweet any amount to other Twitter users he deems worthy of @Bob tokens. That Tweet would be a simple command like @cryptoraves send 2000 @JaneDoe. Then our system would detect it, process it, and respond with: cryptoraves says, You sent @JaneDoe 2000 @Bob tokens.  Now Twitter users can simply browse on their favorite social media site as they normally do, but with another trick up their sleeve.  Blockchain technology is now accessible to the general public on an already familiar platform, and introduces them to the idea of a tokenized economy.

What we're building

Our website would offer a page for every users portfolio of tokens to be viewed and admired.  We see this as a fun way for users to RAVE about online content; to upvote, like, or re-tweet in a more meaningful way; and begin to see the vision of the blockchain in action. Other use cases include businesses using cryptoraves tokens as coupons or rewards points, and organizations marketing their tokens as a way to secure funding.
